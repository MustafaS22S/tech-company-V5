Ultrasonic Distance Sensor

600.00 EGP

An Ultrasonic Distance Sensor measures the distance between the sensor and an object using sound waves. It emits high-frequency sound waves and then measures the time it takes for the waves to bounce back from the object. This time is then converted into a distance measurement. Ultrasonic sensors are commonly used in applications such as obstacle detection in robotics, parking sensors in vehicles, and level sensing in tanks.
