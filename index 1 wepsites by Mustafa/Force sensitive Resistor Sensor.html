A Force Sensitive Resistor (FSR) sensor is a type of sensor that measures the force or pressure applied to its surface. The resistance of the sensor decreases as the force applied increases, allowing it to measure varying levels of pressure. FSR sensors are commonly used in applications such as robotics, wearable devices, and interactive systems where touch or pressure detection is needed.
