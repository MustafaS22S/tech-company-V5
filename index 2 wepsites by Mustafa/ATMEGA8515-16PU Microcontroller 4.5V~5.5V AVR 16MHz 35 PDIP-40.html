ATMEGA8515-16PU Microcontroller 4.5V~5.5V AVR 16MHz 35 PDIP-40

The ATMEGA8515-16PU is an 8-bit AVR microcontroller with a 16MHz clock speed, operating in a voltage range of 4.5V to 5.5V. It comes in a 40-pin PDIP package and features 16KB of Flash memory, 1KB of SRAM, and 512 bytes of EEPROM. It includes 32 I/O pins, multiple timers, PWM channels, and communication interfaces like SPI, UART, and I2C. This microcontroller is suitable for embedded applications, control systems, and DIY projects that require medium processing power and peripheral support.
