The 74HC154 is a 4-to-16 Decoder/Demultiplexer IC designed for digital signal routing and selection. It takes 4 input lines to activate one of 16 output lines, supporting high-speed operation with low power consumption. The IC is compatible with TTL and CMOS logic and operates over a wide voltage range. It is commonly used in memory address decoding, data distribution, and multiplexing applications.
